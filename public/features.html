Deploy Instantly Across Your Organization

One-Click Deployment Across Your Enterprise

As a Google Workspace Admin, deploy Caprome to
every device in your organization with just one click. No
more waiting - the app is instantly installed on all
ChromeOS devices.

Seamless Startup Integration

Always Ready: Force Start with
ChromeOS

Ensure Caprome is always running by setting it to start
automatically with the operating system. No need for
users to manually launch the app - it's always
ready when they are.

Uninterrupted Recording, Every Time

Lock It Down: Prevent Unauthorized Closures

Control is in your hands - restrict users from closing the
Caprome app, ensuring that critical recordings are
never interrupted.

Capture Every Moment, Across All Screens

Capture It All: Multi-Screen Recording

Caprome records every action on every screen
whenever the PC is on. With support for multiple screens,
nothing goes unnoticed.

Tailored Viewing with Advanced User Management

Precision Permissions: Control Who
Sees What

With advanced user management, decide who can view
and comment on recordings. Perfect for coaching and
feedback without compromising privacy.

Insights at Your Fingertips

Visualize the Data: In-App Analysis Tools

Dive deep into the data with in-app analysis of user
activities, including mouse clicks and movements.
Visualize productivity and spot trends in real-time.

Stay Alert with AI-Driven Insights

AI Alerts: Real-Time Distraction
Detection

Powered by Google Gemini, Caprome analyzes live
recordings and alerts admins when users are distracted,
ensuring optimal focus and productivity.

Monitor Live for Instant Oversight

Eyes On: Live Screen Monitoring

With live monitoring, admins can view the screens of
agents, employees, or users in real-time, providing
instant oversight and the ability to intervene if needed.

Trust and Security

Storage options : Ensuring Data
Accessibility

Keep all your valuable data securely within your own
storage solutions, such as Google Drive, OneDrive, GCP
Storage, Azure Storage, or any other preferred option.
Your data stays protected and easily accessible within
the storage system you trust.
